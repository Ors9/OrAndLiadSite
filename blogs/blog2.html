🍖 5 Superfoods Your Dog Will Love

Looking for a way to boost your dog’s health? These 5 dog-friendly superfoods are safe, delicious, and full of nutrients your pup will love.

1. 🫐 Blueberries

Rich in antioxidants, fiber, and vitamins C & K, blueberries are a healthy treat in moderation. Try freezing them on hot days!

2. 🥕 Carrots

Great for your dog’s teeth and eyesight, carrots are low in calories and high in fiber and beta-carotene.

3. 🐟 Salmon

Cooked salmon is a great source of omega-3 fatty acids, which promote a healthy coat, skin, and brain function.

4. 🍌 Bananas

Full of potassium and fiber, bananas are a sweet and soft snack your dog will enjoy — but keep servings small due to sugar.

5. 🥬 Spinach

Spinach is packed with iron and antioxidants. Lightly steam it before serving, and use it as a topper on your dog’s meal.
