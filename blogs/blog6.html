✈️ Traveling with Your Dog

Taking your dog on vacation or a road trip? With a bit of planning, traveling with your furry friend can be safe and stress-free.

🚗 Road Trips

  • Use a secure crate or seatbelt harness.
  • Make frequent stops for bathroom breaks and water.
  • Never leave your dog alone in a parked car.

🛫 Air Travel

If flying, check airline policies in advance. Some allow small dogs in the cabin while others require pets to fly in cargo.

  • Visit your vet for a travel health certificate.
  • Use an airline-approved carrier and label it clearly.
  • Get your dog used to the carrier beforehand.

🏨 Hotel Tips

Book pet-friendly accommodations. Bring your dog’s bed, toys, and familiar items to help them feel at ease in new surroundings.

Whether you’re heading across town or across the country, your pup can be a great travel companion with the right prep!

⚠️ This article is for informational purposes only and does not replace veterinary or travel-related guidance. Always consult with professionals before planning trips with your dog.
