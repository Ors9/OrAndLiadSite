🐶 Puppy Training 101

Welcoming a new puppy is exciting — but also a big responsibility. Early training helps build good behavior and strengthens your bond.

🏠 Housebreaking Basics

Start potty training as early as possible. Take your pup out after eating, sleeping, or playing. Be patient and consistent — praise works better than punishment.

🗣️ Basic Commands

Teach simple commands like "sit", "stay", and "come" using treats and repetition. Keep training sessions short (5–10 minutes) and fun.

🐕 Socialization

Expose your puppy to different people, dogs, and environments early on. This helps prevent fear and aggression later in life.

🧠 Keep It Positive

  • Use rewards like treats, toys, or affection.
  • Never yell or hit — it creates fear and confusion.
  • Stay calm and patient, even if mistakes happen.

Early training sets the foundation for a happy, well-behaved dog. Keep things positive and consistent, and your pup will thrive!
