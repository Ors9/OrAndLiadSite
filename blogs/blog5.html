🚶 Daily Walks: How Long is Enough?

Walks aren’t just for bathroom breaks — they’re essential for your dog’s physical and mental well-being. But how much walking does your dog really need?

🐾 General Guidelines

Most dogs benefit from 30 minutes to 2 hours of walking per day, depending on age, breed, and health.

📏 Factors That Matter

  • Breed: High-energy breeds (like Border Collies or Huskies) may need more exercise.
  • Age: Puppies and seniors often need shorter, more frequent walks.
  • Weather: Adjust walk time in hot or cold conditions.

🎾 Make Walks Enriching

Let your dog sniff and explore. Bring toys, use varied routes, and incorporate basic training for mental stimulation.

Consistency is key! Even short, daily walks help reduce anxiety, manage weight, and strengthen your bond.

⚠️ This article is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for advice from your veterinarian. Always consider your dog’s individual health and needs when planning exercise routines.
