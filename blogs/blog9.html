🍗 Homemade Dog Treat Recipes

Want to spoil your pup with wholesome, tasty snacks? Try these easy homemade treat recipes using simple ingredients.

🍌 Banana-Oat Cookies

Mash 1 ripe banana with 1 cup oats. Form into small balls and bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes.

🥜 Peanut Butter Bites

Mix 1/2 cup unsweetened peanut butter, 1 cup pumpkin puree, and 1.5 cups oat flour. Roll and refrigerate.

🧊 Frozen Yogurt Cubes

Blend plain yogurt with blueberries and freeze in silicone molds for a summer treat.

Always check with your vet before introducing new ingredients, and avoid xylitol and added sugars.
