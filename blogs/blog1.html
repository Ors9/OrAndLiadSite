🛁 How to Bathe Your Dog the Right Way

Bath time can be a bonding experience — or a struggle! Knowing how and when to bathe your dog makes all the difference.

🧼 How Often Should You Bathe?

Most dogs only need a bath every 4–6 weeks. Overbathing can dry out their skin, while too little might lead to odor or dirt buildup.

🚿 What Shampoo Should I Use?

Always use a shampoo made specifically for dogs. Human shampoos can irritate their skin. For sensitive pups, look for an oatmeal or hypoallergenic formula.

🐾 Bathing Tips for Success

  • Brush your dog before bathing to remove tangles.
  • Use lukewarm water – not too hot or cold.
  • Place a towel or non-slip mat in the tub for grip.
  • Use treats and a calm voice to ease anxiety.
  • Rinse thoroughly to avoid leftover shampoo.

With the right tools and a little patience, bath time can be easy and even enjoyable!
