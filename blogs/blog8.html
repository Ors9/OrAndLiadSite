🐾 Understanding Dog Body Language

Your dog is constantly "talking" — you just need to learn how to listen. Understanding body language helps build trust and prevent misunderstandings.

👀 Eyes, Ears, and Tail

  • Relaxed eyes and ears usually signal a calm dog.
  • Pinned ears or a tucked tail can indicate fear or submission.
  • Stiff posture or a high, wagging tail may show alertness or arousal.

🐕 Common Signals

Yawning and lip licking can be signs of stress. Play bows invite fun, while growling means “back off.”

🤝 Build Better Communication

Pay attention to context and combine body language with vocal cues. Your awareness helps keep interactions positive and safe.
