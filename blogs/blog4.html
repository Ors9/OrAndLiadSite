🏠 Creating the Perfect Dog-Friendly Home

Your home should be a safe, comfortable place for your dog to thrive. With a few thoughtful adjustments, you can make it both beautiful and pup-friendly.

🛋️ Safe Spaces

Designate a cozy corner or bed area just for your dog. It helps them feel secure and gives them a place to rest when things get busy.

🍽️ Food & Water Zones

Set up clean, easily accessible spots for food and water. Use mats to catch spills and choose bowls that are the right size for your dog.

🚫 Dog-Proofing Essentials

  • Keep wires, medications, and cleaning supplies out of reach.
  • Use baby gates if needed to limit access to certain rooms.
  • Secure trash cans and food storage areas.

🐕 Play and Enrichment

Provide plenty of toys, puzzle feeders, and chew options. Mental stimulation is just as important as physical activity.

A dog-friendly home doesn’t require a full renovation. With small changes and a little planning, you’ll create a space where your pup can feel truly at home.
