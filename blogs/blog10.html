💤 Dog Sleep 101: How Much Rest Do They Need?

Dogs sleep a lot more than humans — and that’s perfectly normal! But how much is enough, and what affects their sleep habits?

⏱️ Typical Sleep Needs

  • Puppies: up to 18–20 hours/day
  • Adult dogs: 12–14 hours/day
  • Seniors: often more than 14 hours/day

🛏️ Creating a Sleep-Friendly Space

Provide a quiet, cozy bed in a draft-free spot. Avoid disruptions and maintain a consistent routine.

⚠️ Signs of Sleep Issues

Excessive restlessness, loud snoring, or frequent wake-ups may signal discomfort or health issues. Talk to your vet if you're concerned.
